What is Yu-Gi-Oh?

  • It's a popular Japanese anime and manga franchise from Kazuki Takahashi that mainly involves characters who play a card game called Duel Monsters

  • At the start, there were only two card types: Monster & Magic, where a monster battle result relied on monsters Attack and Defense Points and Magic Card effects.

  • According to the author, the game was designed as such because he felt that Magic the Gathering rules were too complicated, and he wanted to create something similar but simpler.

Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game (TCG)

The best-selling card game of all time according to Guinness World Records, with over 22 billion cards sold as of August 2009. The TCG is played worldwide, but mostly in North America, Europe, Latin America and Australia.

Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game (Abbreviated as "OCG") is the version of the Yu-Gi-Oh! card game manufactured by Konami.
